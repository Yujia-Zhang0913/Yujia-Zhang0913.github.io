Project

DriveGPT4: Interpretable End-to-end Autonomous Driving via Large Language Model
July 2023-Oct. 2023
Advisor: Prof.Hengshuang Zhao(HKU)

  • Developed a new visual instruction tuning dataset for interpretable autonomous driving.
  • Present a novel multimodal LLM called DriveGPT4, based on Valley
  • Evaluate all methods on multiple tasks, and DriveGPT4 outperforms all baselines. In addition, DriveGPT4 can handle unseen scenarios with zero-shot generalization.

Control of Pneumatic Articial Muscles with Cerebellar-like Model
Apr. 2022-Present
Advisor: Prof. Qinyuan Ren(ZJU)

  • Completed the antagonistic control simulation of Mckibben-type artificial muscle in matlab and perfected the method of making Mckibben-type artificial muscle.
  • A Mckibben-type artificial muscle control platform with proportional valve as the main pneumatic actuator has been built, which can realize multiple muscle control;
  • Completed the movement of antagonistic muscles in the physical simplification system, and controlled the movement of joints attached to three muscles;
  • Built the matlab antagonistic pair model and SNN control model and CMAC will be considered to control the three muscles in the future;

A Force Feedback Control Method Based on MPC and Learning Method of a Rigid-Flexible Continuum Robot
Dec. 2022-Present
Advisor: Prof. Haojian Lu and Prof. Rong Xiong(ZJU)

  • Built a continuum manipulator platform with 9 cardan joints, which can move towards different directions;
  • Figured out the kinematics of Continuum Robot

Modeling and Control of Space Manipulator
