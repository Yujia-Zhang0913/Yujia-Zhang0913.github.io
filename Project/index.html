Control of Pneumatic Articial Muscles with Cerebellar-like Model

Apr. 2022-Present

Advisor: Prof. Qinyuan Ren(ZJU)

  • Completed the antagonistic control simulation of Mckibben-type artificial muscle in matlab and perfected the method of making Mckibben-type artificial muscle.
